Frequently Asked Questions

Music Submissions

Send your best 2–3 tracks (links only — no attachments) to ar@torchmusicgroup.com. Include your name, city, social media links, and a brief bio. We listen to every submission. If it’s a fit, we’ll reach out within 2–4 weeks.

We look for artists who are committed to craft, have a clear identity, and are building something that lasts. Genre is secondary — we prioritize intention, authenticity, and work ethic. We sign artists, not trends.

Yes. Producers and songwriters can submit to the same email. Include credits, a brief description of your sound, and links to your work. We’re always looking for collaborators who match our standard.

Business & Partnerships

Full-service artist management, A&R, creative direction, release strategy, brand development, and access to the Torch ATL recording estate. Every deal is structured around ownership, transparency, and long-term artist growth.

For sync inquiries, contact sync@torchmusicgroup.com with details about your project, placement type, and timeline. We’ll respond with available catalog options.

For partnerships, sponsorships, or business inquiries, email info@torchmusicgroup.com. We’re open to strategic collaborations that align with our brand and mission.

Careers & Internships

Yes. We run internship programs in A&R, marketing, studio operations, and artist management. Open positions are listed in the Careers section above. You can also send a general inquiry to careers@torchmusicgroup.com.

Torch Music Group is headquartered in Atlanta, Georgia. Our recording estate, Torch ATL, operates as a private, membership-only creative facility in the Atlanta metro area.
